# Transfer Track Roadmap

## Near-term improvements
- Add a simple Supabase backend for profiles and saved interest requests
- Add professor filters by research area, transfer readiness, and location
- Introduce richer profile fields such as GPA, target universities, and timeline
- Add a real opportunity feed with campus and lab partners

## Mid-term features
- Verified professor accounts and public faculty profiles
- Community college transfer planning tools with coursework mapping
- AI-assisted outreach messages and research email drafts
- Career readiness workflows with resume and interview guidance

## Long-term vision
- University and community college partnerships for transfer pathways
- Real-time research project matching with faculty mentors
- A centralized transfer research ecosystem for transfer student success
- Data-driven dashboards for transfer student outcomes and research placement
