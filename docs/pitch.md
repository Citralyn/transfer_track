# Transfer Track Pitch

## Problem
Community college students often struggle to identify university professors and research paths that match their transfer goals. They need a simple way to connect coursework, interests, and transferable skills with faculty research opportunities.

## Solution
Transfer Track is a lightweight demo that helps students discover professors, research opportunities, and course prep resources before transferring. It uses local profile data and rule-based matching to surface relevant recommendations and next steps.

## Target users
- Community college students planning a transfer
- Transfer students exploring research-ready majors
- Students interested in research mentorship and career preparation

## Demo flow
1. Landing page introduces the app and prompts students to begin.
2. Onboarding collects student profile details, coursework, interests, and goals.
3. The dashboard shows recommended professors, research opportunities, resources, and a readiness checklist.
4. Students can view professor details and express interest in research.
5. Professor dashboard shows saved local interest requests.

## Impact
This demo makes transfer planning more concrete by combining academic preparation with research opportunity discovery. It helps students move from general goals to specific faculty connections and prep actions.
