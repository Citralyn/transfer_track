# Transfer Track Demo Script

## 2-minute demo

1. Open the app to the landing page and explain the goal: helping transfer students find professors, research opportunities, and preparation resources.
2. Click the start button and complete the onboarding form as a fictional student named Maya, attending South Valley College and planning to transfer into computer science.
3. Show how completed courses, interests, and skills generate a personalized dashboard with recommended professors and research opportunities.
4. Point out the course prep resources and readiness checklist, which highlight the next steps for missing prerequisites.
5. Visit a professor detail page to review their research areas, prerequisites, resources, and related opportunities.
6. Click "Express Interest" to save a local request and explain how the professor dashboard collects student interest without a real backend.
7. Open the professor dashboard to prove the demo flow works end to end with mock data and localStorage.

## Fictional story

Maya is a community college student who wants to transfer into a four-year computer science program. She has completed Python and calculus courses, enjoys education technology and AI, and wants to find a mentor who accepts transfer students. Transfer Track helps her identify a research mentor, understand what she still needs to study, and express interest in a faculty research opportunity.
